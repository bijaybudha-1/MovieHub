Privacy Policy

Your Privacy is Our Priority

1. Information Collection

MovieHub is a client-side application. We do not collect or store your personal data on our servers. Any "user profile" information, watchlists, or ratings you create are stored exclusively in your browser's LocalStorage.

2. How We Use Data

The data stored in your browser is used solely to provide personalized features like your Watchlist, Favorites, and Rating History. We do not have access to this data, and it is never shared with third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

We fetch cinematic metadata from the TMDB API. When you browse MovieHub, your browser makes direct requests to TMDB servers. Please refer to TMDB's Privacy Policy for more information on how they handle data.

4. Cookies

MovieHub uses LocalStorage as an alternative to cookies for maintaining your dashboard state. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's site data or cache.

5. Cookies and Tracking

While we don't use server-side cookies, we use browser session storage to remember your theme preferences and login status. This data never leaves your device.

6. Third-Party Links

Our service may contain links to external sites (like IMDb or YouTube). We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these third-party websites.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard best practices for our frontend security. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Protect your device to keep your local data safe.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. The latest version will always be available on this page with the "Last Updated" date.
